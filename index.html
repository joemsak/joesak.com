Thank you for taking the time to get to know me today. My 20 year background includes both front-end and back-end programming, while nurturing a keen eye and strong passion for a well-designed UI and UX. I prefer working prototypes and designing in the browser over plans and mockups. I like weekly iterative cycles, I abhor recurring meetings, and I love human-centered design.

design is not what something looks like, but how it works.

It's not just visuals—or products—that are the only things that need design. Everything that humans must do to accomplish their goals should be carefully designed. How we work, conduct support, plan, write, and organize our team, are crucial areas we should measure and evaluate to deliver higher quality.

We must be willing to trim away everything that's unecessary, be honest with ourselves about what's not working, and promote empathy, compassion, and humans above policy, routine, and expectation.
